Why do we use an ad shortener?

To work full-time on Galaxy Swapper while keeping it entirely free, we utilize an Ad-Shortener. This allows us to earn money for the time invested in this project.

What is Lootlink?

Lootlink is an Ad-Shortener service that supports Galaxy Swapper to remain fully free while enabling our full-time commitment to the project through pay-per-click/install revenue.
